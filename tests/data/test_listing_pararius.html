    Description

    Beautiful light, fully furnished 3 bedroom apartment in one of the most popular neighborhoods of Amsterdam-West, on the edge of the Baarsjes, at the Postjeskade. This particularly attractive apartment of approx. 86 m2 is located on the third floor of a charming building with its own balcony and a communal roof terrace.
    The apartment is very suitable to share with a maximum of 2 people, available for 12 months.

    In the immediate vicinity there are plenty of nice cafés and restaurants on and near Mercatorplein such as Frits, Café Zurich, restaurant Edel and restaurant Moer. Vondelpark and Rembrandtpark are less than 5 minutes' walk away. You can do your daily shopping around the corner on Jan Evertsenstraat. The "Jan Eef" has enough stores, from an Amsterdam baker to a Turkish store.

    Layout:
    Central entrance with stairs to the second floor. Hall with access to all rooms. Bright living room at the rear with luxury open kitchen. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, freezer, oven, microwave, dishwasher and an induction hob with extractor. From the living room access to a spacious bedroom / study at the rear and access to the balcony. The parents' bedroom and 2nd bedroom are located on the front. The modern bathroom has a bath with shower, washbasin, toilet and a bidet. The washing machine and dryer are placed in a separate room.

    Particularities:

    • Fully furnished
      *Available at least for 12 months with option to extend
    • Conveniently located to public transport and green areas

    Access: Within 3 minutes you are with the car on the Ring A10. Lelylaan and Sloterdijk stations are close by and various public transport lines to Zuid WTC station, central station via Leidseplein or Jordaan. By bike you are within 5 minutes at the ten Kate market, the Hallen, Hoofddorpplein or Amstelveenseweg and within 10 minutes at the Concertgebouw, Leidseplein or Jordaan
